About this data

This brief summarizes community-identified hotspot locations recorded during participatory mapping activities in Malawi and Zambia districts. Participant group labels come from the contributed source files, and severity scores describe the relative concern recorded for each hotspot.

The dataset was loaded from the project location records and may not include every community, season, or local condition. Treat the results as planning evidence to review with communities and technical teams before selecting interventions.
